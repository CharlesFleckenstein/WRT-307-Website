When AI isn't enough
Escalation to a Live Agent

Some issues require a human touch. Here's when and how the system escalates your request to a support professional.

Escalation is triggered when:
🔀 The issue involves a complex or unusual circumstance outside the AI's scope.
❓ The AI cannot understand the user's problem after multiple attempts.
🙋 The user requests a live agent directly.

What happens during escalation:

🔔

Transfer Notification

An automated message appears: "Transferring to live representative." You don't need to do anything.

⏳

Wait Page Loads

A wait page shows your estimated hold time. The system automatically shares your full chat log with the incoming agent so you don't have to repeat yourself.

🧑‍💼

Agent Joins the Chat

The live agent introduces themselves. They will be clearly labeled as a Live Agent so you always know who you're talking to.

✅

Issue Resolved & Session Closed

Once your issue is resolved, either party can close the session. You'll be redirected back to the main support page.

ℹ️ Your Chat History is Shared Automatically When a live agent joins, they already have full context from your conversation with the AI — so you won't need to repeat yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions

Click any question to expand the answer.

What if the chatbot doesn't understand my request? ▼
Try rephrasing your request with more specific details — include your order number, account email, or product name. If the issue continues, type "live agent" to be connected to a support professional.
How do I connect to a live agent? ▼
Type "live agent" into the chat at any time, or select the "Talk to a Representative" option that appears in the chat interface. You will be transferred as soon as an agent is available.
Is this system available 24/7? ▼
Yes — the AI chatbot is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Live human agents are available during standard business hours. If you request a live agent outside business hours, the AI will assist you until an agent becomes available.
Will the live agent know what I've already told the chatbot? ▼
Yes. When your conversation is transferred, the live agent receives a full log of your chat history with the AI. You won't need to repeat yourself or start over.
Do I need an account to use the chat? ▼
No account is required. Simply open the chat and start typing your question — no login or sign-up needed.
What kinds of issues can the AI handle? ▼
The AI is trained to help with order tracking, FAQs, returns, account password resets, and other common support questions. For complex, unusual, or sensitive issues, it will automatically escalate to a live agent.
